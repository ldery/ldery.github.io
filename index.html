Intro

I am a Research Engineer at Facebook AI Research (FAIR) in Seattle. I am currently broadly interested in text generation from structured data, robust evaluation of dialogue systems and memory based approaches to dialogue. I have previously worked in Computer Vision - specifically CV algorithms for High Energy Physics (HEP) and Video Understanding. I did my undergrad (Physics) and Masters (Computer Science) at Stanford University and will be starting a PhD in Computer Science @ Carnegie Mellon in the Fall. Outside of A.I, I am very passionate about Music, Art , Neuroscience, Philosophy and Food !!.
