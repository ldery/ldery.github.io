Intro

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon Univeristy co-advised by Ameet Talwakar and Graham Neubig. Before starting my PhD, I was Research Engineer at Facebook AI Research (FAIR) in Seattle. My current research interests broadly covers Meta-Learning situated in the context of Natural Language Processing. I have previously worked in Computer Vision - specifically CV algorithms for High Energy Physics (HEP) and Video Understanding. I did my undergrad (Physics) and Masters (Computer Science) at Stanford University. Outside of A.I, I am very passionate about Music, Art , Neuroscience, Philosophy and Food !!.
